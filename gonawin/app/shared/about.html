About gonawin

A social web application to make "friendly bets" on sports events.

We believe that today's betting platforms are full of ads that polute the game itself. We want to create a platform that people can use to bet/play with their friends and family or anyone they want, without any ads and without asking for any money.

You can find the code of this web app in github.com here as well as the description of how we built it and the stack of technologies that we used.

The why behind the Y:


We, Santiago and Remy, built this project from scratch without really knowing how it would turn out but with the will to learn something new. And we did.
Carmen helped us a lot with the design of the app, the logo and character (bidulio) and giving us her opinion concerning the usability and design of the app.

We hope you enjoy playing as much as we enjoyed making it.
